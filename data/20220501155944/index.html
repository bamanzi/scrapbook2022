Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep? is a 24-issue comic book adaptation of Philip K. Dick's 1968 novel of the same name. Published in 2009 by BOOM! Studios and drawn by Tony Parker, it is an unabridged adaptation of the novel, containing all of its narration and dialogue.

During its run, BOOM! Studios also released Dust to Dust, which serves as a prequel to Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?.
