Both come across the two factions in a global fight caused by business and war. 

The other narrative retells stories about Anakin’s doubt of the Jedi Order (as how Qui Gon did) and the probability that Kenobi might leave. Star Wars: The Thrawn Trilogy is a Star Wars adult-fiction novel series that consists of Heir to the Empire, Dark Force Rising, and The Last Command. This original trilogy is set in 9 ABY in the Expanded Universe in a far-away galaxy, five years after the Episode VI Return of the Jedi.  

The original trilogy recounts the Imperial Grand Admiral Thrawn’s campaign and the efforts of the Dark Jedi Joruus C’baoth to claim Luke, Leia, and her children as his apprentices.
